Join A Small Group

A small Group also known as a cell is a group of six to twelve people, who gather each week online or in person.In a small group, you get to hang out with like-minded believers, study the word of God, pray together, share testimonies and much more.
At Dominion City church we have over fifty small groups within different locations in the country
